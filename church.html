Journal Entry #1: Galilean Fellowship


On November 19, 2024, the Galilean Fellowship, this event was supposed to be held at Amphitheater but was held in Ausejo Hall instead because of the rain. This event is one of the many events part of UCLEM, organized by USLC and spearheaded by the pastoral ministry of the SU Church. For this activity, I am just a normal participant as this is a requirement for my CHS.

My initial expectations for this activity was filled with uncertainty as I didn’t know what a fellowship is in general, but the more time I spent in this activity the more I understood what this activity means. During this activity, we were all discussing on what fears we had and how to overcome them using our faith in God. My favorite part of this activity was when my classmates were re-enacting the David & Goliath story, which is what this UCLEM’s theme is based on which is “Faith Over Fear: Facing Our Giants”.

After this activity, I have learned how important our faith is to God. If you put your faith in God, he will help you in literally anything, even in things that seem impossible to do. I hope that my fellow classmates will also put their faith God so that they can receive help in whatever giants their facing right now.

Because of this activity, I am putting all my faith to God.

Journal Entry #4: Meditation: Thriving Through Adversities


On October 23, 2024, an event at SU Church named “Meditation: Thriving Through Adversities” happened. This church event is meant to prepare students for the midterm exams happening next week. For this event, I am just normal participant.

The theme for this mass is “Thriving Through Adversities”, the adversities in question are the typhoon were experiencing at that time, and the midterms exams next week, but it’s not limited to just that, an adversity can be anything that can trouble you, anywhere at any time. During this event, we read a few bible scriptures such as Ephesians 4:12-13 and James 1:1-4, which fits perfectly with the theme “Thriving Through Adversities”, because these bible verses can help us thrive through adversities. My favorite part of this event was a testimony of faith from Vin Trixter Gunot, a 1st year BSIT student and the current president of DOST SA SU, their testimony of faith is that as long you put your faith into God, you will be able to go thrive through any trials, battles, tribulations, and adversities, and I wholeheartedly agree with this statement.

After this activity, I feel more relaxed and more motivated to study for my midterm exams next week. I hope that my faith in God and my own effort in thriving through adversities can help me pass my midterms exams. And I hope the other people in the church with me can thrive through whatever adversities they’re currently facing right now.

Because of this activity, I am ready to take on my midterm exams.
