Journal Entry #5: Balik Talent Lecture: “Big Data and IoT: Why They Matter in Innovation”


On August 20, 2024, a Balik Talent Lecture was hosted at ATA hall. The speaker for this lecture was Mr. Abraham Johannes T. Tourbier, a senior data manager. For this lecture, I’m just a normal participant as this lecture is required.

My initial expectations for this lecture was uncertainty as I have no idea what Big Data and IoT is, but I was intrigued to know what it is exactly as this knowledge could potentially help me to become a game or software developer. During the lecture, the speaker talked about what Big Data exactly is. According to the speaker Big Data is basically a massive volume of structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data generated at high speed from various sources like social media, sensors, transactions, and devices. The speaker also explains what IoT is. IoT refers to a network of interconnected devices, sensors, and systems that communicate and share data over the internet. The part that surprised me the most was when Big Data and IoT was used to control several devices such lights, sensors, and security cameras as showcased in the speaker’s presentation.

After the orientation, I can understand why Big Data and IoT matters in innovation. These technologies can help everyone manage everything much better. I hope these technologies can develop even further and receive more investment so that it can be beneficial for everyone.

Because of this activity, I am knowledgeable about Big Data and IoT and I hope this knowledge can help me when I become a game or software developer.
